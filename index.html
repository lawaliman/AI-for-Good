What We Do

Our organization harnesses cutting-edge robotics to revolutionize disaster response. Our robots are designed to perform crucial tasks in hazardous environments, such as assessing structural damage, locating survivors, and delivering medical supplies.

Our Mission

We aim to empower young innovators by bridging the gap between technology and real-world applications. Through hands-on learning, collaboration, and mentorship, we provide participants with the skills to design and program robots that can save lives.

Rules & Guidelines

Understand the requirements and specifications for participating in the Ultimate Robotics Challenge.

Robots must be fully autonomous and comply with size and safety requirements. Decorations should align with the disaster-response theme.

Teams should consist of 3-5 members, with roles divided among programming, design, and strategy. Collaboration and knowledge of robot operations are mandatory.

Each round will last 2 minutes. Robots must perform assigned tasks within the competition field and avoid damaging any components.
